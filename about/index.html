About me

I’m currently working on the development of Graziashop, a high fashion marketplace member of the Grazia International Network, owned by Mondadori. My role has multiple touch points within the business and it combines strategy, design, technology and user experience.  

Previously I was the web manager of London-Boutiques.com, a company that was accquired by Mondadori to accelerate the development of Graziashop.

Outside of work I’m a husband and a proud father of one child, a SL Benfica fan and a avid cinema and tv shows consumer. Radio control is a long time passion and hobby and I’ve founded the UMDEZ community. I've also organised more than 25 events, including the Portuguese Off-Road Series. As a racer I’m a multiple A final participant and winner, and the 2012 Stock Touring Car Portuguese Vice-Champion.
